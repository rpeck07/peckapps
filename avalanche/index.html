Know what's actually urgent.

Most students guess which assignments to work on first. Avalanche calculates a danger score for every assignment using a formula that weighs deadline proximity, grade weight, confidence level, and hours remaining.

Color-coded risk levels from green to red so you know at a glance
Updates in real time as deadlines get closer
Factors in logged study hours so scores drop as you make progress

See your week before it hits.

Avalanche projects how many hours of work you have due each day over the next 5 days. Heavy days show in orange. Crunch days show in red. You'll never be blindsided by a brutal Wednesday again.

Estimated hours per day based on your actual assignments

Set up your courses with colors and your current grade in each one. Avalanche uses this to make every calculation accurate to your actual situation.

Enter each assignment with its due date, grade weight, estimated hours, and confidence level. Takes about 20 seconds per assignment.

03

Let Avalanche Navigate

Your dashboard updates in real time. Check your danger scores, workload forecast, and grade targets every day to stay ahead of your semester.
